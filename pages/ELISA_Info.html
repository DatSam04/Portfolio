ELISA

ECS Lab Inventory System App

Team: 21st

Position: Front End Developer

Description: ELISA is an inventory management application that is created to help the ECS Lab Department to keep track of the equipments more efficiently. It uses RFID to scan QR codes and Barcodes to record equipment movement to the system, which makes it easier for the Lab Coordinator to monitor and manage the equipments. ELISA also helps students to reserve any equipments quickly and conveniently.
